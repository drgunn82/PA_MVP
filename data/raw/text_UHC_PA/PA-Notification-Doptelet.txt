1. Background:
Doptelet (avatrombopag) is a thrombopoietin receptor agonist indicated for the treatment of
thrombocytopenia in adult patients with chronic liver disease who are scheduled to undergo a
procedure. Doptelet is also indicated for the treatment of adult patients with chronic immune
thrombocytopenia (ITP) who have had an insufficient response to a previous treatment.
2. Coverage Criteriaa:
A. Thrombocytopenia in patients with chronic liver disease who are scheduled to undergo a
procedure
1. Doptelet will be approved based on all of the following criteria:
a. Diagnosis of thrombocytopenia
-AND-
b. Patient has chronic liver disease
-AND-
c. Patient is scheduled to undergo a procedure
Authorization will be issued for 1 month.
B. Chronic immune thrombocytopenia (ITP)
1. Initial Authorization
a. Doptelet will be approved based on both of the following criteria
(1) Diagnosis of chronic immune thrombocytopenia (ITP)
-AND-
(2) Patient has had an insufficient response to a previous treatment (e.g.,
corticosteroids, immunoglobulins, thrombopoietin receptor agonists, splenectomy)
