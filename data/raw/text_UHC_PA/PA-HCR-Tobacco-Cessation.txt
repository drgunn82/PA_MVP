1. Background:
Tobacco cessation therapies are more likely to be successful for patients who are motivated
to stop tobacco use and who are provided additional advice and support. Patients should be
provided with appropriate educational materials and counseling to support the quit attempt.
The patient should set a quit date.
This program is designed to meet Health Care Reform requirements for tobacco cessation
coverage at zero dollar cost share.
