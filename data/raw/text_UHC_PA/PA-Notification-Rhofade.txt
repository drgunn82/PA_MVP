1. Background:
Mirvaso (brimonidine) 0.33% topical gel and Rhofade (oxymetazoline) 1% topical cream are
alpha-adrenergic agonists indicated for the topical treatment of persistent (nontransient)
erythema of rosacea in adults.
2. Coverage Criteriaa:
A. Initial Authorization
1. Mirvaso or Rhofade will be approved based on the following criterion:
a. Diagnosis of rosacea
Authorization will be issued for 12 months.
B. Reauthorization
1. Mirvaso or Rhofade will be approved based on the following criterion:
a. Documentation of positive clinical response to therapy.
Authorization will be issued for 12 months.
