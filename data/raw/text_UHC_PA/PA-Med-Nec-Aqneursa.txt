1. Background
Aqneursa (glycerol phenylbutyrate) is indicated for the treatment of neurological manifestations
of Niemann-Pick disease type C (NPC) in adults and pediatric patients weighing ≥15 kg.
2. Coverage Criteriaa:
A. Initial Authorization
1. Aqneursa will be approved based on all of the following criteria:
a. Both of the following:
(1) Diagnosis of Niemann-Pick disease type C (NPC)
-AND-
(2) Diagnosis has been genetically confirmed by mutation analysis of NPC1 and NPC2
genes
-AND-
b. Aqneursa is being used to treat neurological manifestations of NPC
-AND-
c. One of the following:
(1) Aqneursa is prescribed in combination with miglustat
-OR-
(2) History of failure, contraindication, or intolerance to miglustat
-AND-
d. Patient is not receiving Aqneursa in combination with Miplyffa (arimoclomol)
-AND-
e. Aqneursa is prescribed by or in consultation with a provider with expertise in the
