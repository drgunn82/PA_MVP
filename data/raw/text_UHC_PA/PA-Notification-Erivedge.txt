1. Background:
Erivedge® (vismodegib) is a hedgehog pathway inhibitor indicated for the treatment of adults
with metastatic basal cell carcinoma, or with locally advanced basal cell carcinoma that has
recurred following surgery or who are not candidates for surgery, and who are not candidates for
radiation.1
The National Comprehensive Cancer Network (NCCN) also recommends Erivedge for the
treatment of medulloblastoma for recurrence as a single agent in patients who have received
prior chemotherapy and have mutations in the sonic hedgehog pathway.2
